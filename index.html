Hi,  I  am  Vaibhav (pronounced:  Y-bhav).   Welcome   to   my   personal   website!
I am an Engineering Manager specializing in Data Engineering & Bioinformatics, with deep expertise in building enterprise-scale data platforms and AI-driven solutions for drug discovery. I lead cross-functional teams to architect next-generation data fabrics that power biomarker discovery programs and harmonize vast pre-clinical datasets across pharmaceutical research pipelines. I enjoy building & scaling up infrastructures to ingest & process large-complex data to serve responsive & user-friendly applications. I am also experienced in conducting research & productionizing ML-based services. I am passionate about writing clean code & a strong proponent of the guidelines in The Pragmatic Programmer. I also like to contribute to open source projects.

Currently, I work as Engineering Manager: Data & Bioinformatics at Debiopharm based in Lausanne, Switzerland. Previously, I have worked in several engineering roles with enterprises providing services in Genomics, Supply-chain & Internet-of-Things domain. I have a doctorate in Information Systems, where my research was focused on designing Privacy-preserving ML-based Information Systems. During the course of my PhD, I worked on diverse methodological & applied industrial projects, ranging from information theoretic analysis of ML models & building services to address the free-rider problem on public transport networks, in collaboration with Transports Publics Lausannois.
